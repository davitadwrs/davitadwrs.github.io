01 — Profile

A calm chairside, built on six years of practice.

I treat dentistry as a relationship, not a transaction — diagnosing carefully, explaining clearly, and pacing every visit so my patients leave feeling lighter than they came in.

I trained at Trisakti University (Bachelor of Dentistry, 2016; Doctor of Medicine in Dentistry, 2019) and have since practiced across hospitals and private clinics from the Anambas and Batam Islands to Jakarta. My day-to-day spans examinations and pain management, restorative work, supervised root canal therapy, and patient education — with a particular care for children and elderly visitors who need a gentler pace.
